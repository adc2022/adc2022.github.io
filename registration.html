ADC CONFERENCE REGISTRATION

Please register through ACSW registration. Early bird registrations close 8stJanuary 2021.

Important

  • ADC-21 requires at least one ACSW registration per accepted paper, and this registration must be made by 10 January 2018; otherwise the paper will not be included in the proceedings.
  • If you are the co-author of more than one paper, you only need to register once.
