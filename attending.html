Travel information

We are super excited to be able to welcome you to Sydney. Due to current government regulations, all those who attend in person, access the venue or attend any social function must be fully immunised or have a valid exemption. Proof of vaccination will be required by the venue to gain entry. The Conference Managers have no control over this requirement and the onus is on the individual to provide proof of vaccination.

International covid information

From 21 February 2022, all visa holders who are fully vaccinated for international travel purposes can travel to Australia without a travel exemption. Unvaccinated visa holders will still need to be in an exempt category or hold an individual travel exemption to enter Australia. Information regarding the current travel restrictions for international travellers available at https://covid19.homeaffairs.gov.au/travel-restrictions
Information regarding travel and border requirements for international travellers wishing to travel to Australia is available at https://covid19.homeaffairs.gov.au/coming-australia
Information regarding what international travellers need before travelling is available at https://covid19.homeaffairs.gov.au/before-i-travel
Information regarding the Australian travel declaration is available at https://covid19.homeaffairs.gov.au/australia-travel-declaration

The University of New South Wales
ADC 2022 will be held in the Design Studio, Level 5, Ainsworth Building (J17), Kensington Campus (Main Campus), The University of New South Wales.


UNSW's main campus is located on a 38 hectare site in the suburb of Kensington. Every weekday Kensington Campus generates more than 60,000 trips to and from work and study making it the largest single destination for people in the Eastern Suburbs.
